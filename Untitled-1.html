Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg Chicken
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 3 Onions (sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 2 tbsp Biryani Masala
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • 500g Rice (soaked)
  • 1/2 cup Oil
  • 1/2 cup Mint Leaves
  • 1/2 cup Coriander Leaves
  • Saffron or Yellow Food Color (in 2 tbsp milk)

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and fry onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for a minute.
  3. Add chicken and cook until it changes color.
  4. Add yogurt, tomatoes, and all spices. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Boil rice 70% done and drain.
  6. Layer rice and chicken in a pot.
  7. Top with mint, coriander, and saffron milk.
  8. Cover and cook on low heat (dum) for 15-20 minutes.
  9. Serve hot!
